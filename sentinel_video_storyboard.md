## Recording tips

- **Resolution:** Record at whatever your screen native is. Export at 1080p minimum.
- **Cursor:** Hide the cursor between actions. Show it when clicking to make the action legible.
- **Pace:** Don't rush the clips. 5 seconds feels short while recording but is fine in the edit.
- **Order:** Record all the clips first, then drop in the text cards. The cards can be made in any simple editor (Keynote, Figma, even Canva).
- **Text card style:** Match the Codex video — Inter or SF Pro, black on white, no shadows, no decoration.
- **No music needed** unless you want something very quiet underneath. The Codex video uses ambient sound.
- **Transitions:** Cut directly. No fades between clips. Fade in/out on the text cards only.
